​1st February, 2024 

​Agdrive Unveils 2024 Program Under The Theme: Feeding The Final Frontier  

​ ​Agdrive is an innovation and investment platform committed to revolutionalize Africa’s agribusiness, food manufacturing, and agritech sector. This dynamic program is scheduled to run from early February to November 2024. The mission of Agdrive is to help “Transform Afica’s Food Future” by fostering innovation, investment, and technical assistance with a focus on the Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa (COMESA) and Southern African Development Community (SADC) markets. 

​ 

​Through a multi-initiative strategy, the Agdrive 2024 program comprises three key components: 

  1. ​Agdrive Xplo Southern Africa Investment and Trade Mission (21st – 26th July, 2024): Bringing together participants from COMESA, SADC, Brazil, China, Netherlands, the United Kingdom, and the United States.  

  1. ​Agdrive Food and Agritech Investment Summit (24th – 25th July, 2024): The summit will run parallel to the trade mission offering a prime opportunity for stakeholders to engage, collaborate, and explore the latest trends in the agribusiness sector. 

  1. ​Agdrive Plus – Advisory and Technical Assistance (April – June 2024, August – November 2024): A two-part initiative providing crucial advisory and support services, strategically scheduled before and after the trade mission. 

​ 

​The Agdrive 2024 program is presented by Drive Africa, AgriEn, and OneLife Communications & Events Company, with support from a collaborative network of government investment agencies, investors, advisory firms, research organizations, development finance institutions, and industry associations.  ​

​The official launch event of the Agdive 2024 program is scheduled for 11th March, 2024, in Lusaka, Zambia. Agdrive announces that pre-registration is already underway and official registration for the program will officially open on 19th February, 2024 and will run up to 12th April, 2024.
